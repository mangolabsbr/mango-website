The rule of three is the most useful formula most people forgot after school. Scaling a recipe, converting a price per kilo, adjusting paint mixtures — it's all the same proportion.

## One screen, one gesture

Proportion shows four numbers in a grid. You fill three, it solves the fourth. There is no mode picker, no category list, no "recipe mode" versus "price mode" — because mathematically there's no difference, and pretending there is would just add taps.

## Keyboards are the interface

Since the whole app is entering numbers, we spent most of our design time on the number pad: big keys, instant recalculation on every digit, and no confirm button. The answer is just there, updating as you type.

It's the least glamorous app in our catalog and one of the most used. Utility wins.

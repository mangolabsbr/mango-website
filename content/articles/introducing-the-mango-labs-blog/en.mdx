Mango Labs now has a blog. This is where we'll write about how we build our apps — the design decisions, the trade-offs, and the occasional lesson learned the hard way.

## What to expect

We build small, focused tools: a currency converter, a bill splitter, a route planner. The articles here will match that spirit — short, concrete, and about real decisions in real products. No growth-hacking listicles, no filler.

Some things we're planning to write about:

- **Behind the apps.** Why Xchanger, Splitte, SunRouter, and Proportion work the way they do.
- **Studio notes.** What it's like shipping to two app stores as a tiny independent team.
- **Engineering choices.** The stack, the constraints we choose on purpose, and what we refuse to build.

## Available in three languages

Like everything on this site, every article is published in English, Spanish, and Portuguese. Pick your language in the header and the whole blog follows.

If there's something you'd like us to write about — or you just want to say hi — [get in touch](/contact). See you in the next post.

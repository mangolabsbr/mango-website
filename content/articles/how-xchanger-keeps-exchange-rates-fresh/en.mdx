Currency rates change every second, but your phone shouldn't have to work that hard. Xchanger's job is to give you a trustworthy number instantly — even when you're standing in a street market with no signal.

## Caching with honesty

Xchanger fetches rates when you open the app and keeps the last known table on the device. If you're offline, you still get an answer — along with a timestamp telling you exactly how old it is. We'd rather show you an honest "updated 3 hours ago" than pretend a cached number is live.

## Doing less on purpose

We deliberately don't poll in the background. Background refresh sounds nice until you realize it drains battery for numbers you look at twice a week. The rates update when you actually need them: when the app opens.

Small decisions like these are invisible when they work — and that's the point.
